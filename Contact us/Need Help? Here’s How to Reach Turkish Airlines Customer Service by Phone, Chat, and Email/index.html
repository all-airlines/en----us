For real-time help, Turkish Airlines offers a live chat feature on their website and mobile app, connecting you instantly to a support representative.

Reach out via direct messages on Facebook or Twitter for additional support.

If you are at the airport, visit the Turkish Airlines customer service desk for immediate, in-person assistance with urgent travel matters.
